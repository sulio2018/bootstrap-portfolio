About Me

I have always been the type of person that does not easily fall to conformity. Indeed, if there was a motto for which I could live by is that, change is important in my life.

In trying to live by this motto, at times I have found myself in some very compromising situations. At the same time, however; my constant urge to seek change has also led me to some wonderful experiences.

As I embark on this new adventure of my life that is coding, I hope to enter a world that can provide change for me on a constant basis. Indeed, I am now beginning to realize that this was the correct path for me to take. It is now up to me how far I want to go.
